3. Web Vitals:
Yeh metrics aapki website ki user experience ko measure karte hain. Web Vitals ko Google ne define kiya hai aur yeh help karta hai websites ko optimize karne mein.

LCP (Largest Contentful Paint):

lcp ka matlab hai ki aapki website ka largest content (jaise image, video, ya text block) kitny time  milliseconds 
mein load ho rha hai. LCP ka target hona chahiye 2.5 seconds ya usse kam.Iska matlab hai ki website ka main
content bahut fast load ho raha hai, jo acchi baat hai. agr 2.5 ya osy km he to 

TBT (Total Blocking Time):

0ms ka matlab hai ki website pe koi blocking time nahi hai. Blocking time ka matlab hota hai ki jab browser koi
action perform karta hai aur usse kisi kaam mein rukawat (block) hoti hai. 0ms ka matlab hai ki aapki website smooth 
hai aur koi blockage nahi hai.


CLS (Cumulative Layout Shift) Score:
0 ka matlab hai ki aapki website ka layout bilkul stable hai. Yaani jab aap page load karte hain, 
toh content (text image apni jha sy hil nhi rha) move nahi ho rha . Har element apne fixed place par rehta hai 
aur website load hone ke baad koi unexpected shift nahi hota.

Achha CLS Score (0): Yeh ensure karta hai ki user ko ek smooth experience mil raha hai. Agar content
 shift hota hai, toh user ko irritate ho sakta hai, kyunki wo sochta hai ki webpage properly load nahi ho raha hai.
